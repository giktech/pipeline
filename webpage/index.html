Order Processing


The charts below show the order delivery time for each state (chart 1) and each product category (chart 2). Delivery timelines outside the normal range (0-500 hours) are in red.


Highlighting a part of above chart (i.e. selecting states) chooses product categories that have the biggest delivery times in the chart below


The brush on chart1 (to select states) can be dragged on both edges.It can also be moved horizontally through the span of the chart. Reloading the page will reset both the charts to original form.
