Exploring Market Trends through Purchase Order History

What does purchase order history tell us?

The placement of a purchase order is vital to our business, as it is the first step in our process. All future stages serve to fulfill the order. Better understanding of the first stage will help improve all downstream processes.

Also, purchase orders are entirely driven by the customer. It serves as a lens into customer behavior. If we can move with market, we can better anticpate product trends and customer needs.

Note: This graph is interactive. At any time, hover over a line within the graph to reveal additional information

What are our most popular products?

The graph has been updated to highlight our top 5 most popular products. Popularity was measured by the amount of growth in purchase order volume over the past 12 months. This list includes health and beauty, housewares, furniture decor, auto, and construction tools.

Some interesting insights to draw:

  • Furniture decor is extremely volatile, but at it's peak owned the largest market share
  • Construction tools has seen the most growth, but still represents a small market share
  • Auto products orders are the most consistent and stable of the group
