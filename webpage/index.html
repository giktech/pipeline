Explore Market Trends

What does purchase order history tell us?

Knowing what customers are purchasing provides insight into where the market is headed, allowing us to adjust as a business. The line chart to the left visualizes this data to make it easier to draw these insights and better understand trends.

A line on the chart represents a product category. As an example, we have the line drawn for our health and beauty products. From each data point along the line, you are able to identify the amount of orders made for a given month. In addition, the graph is interactive! At any time, hover over a line within the graph to reveal additional information.

Scroll down to explore further!

What are our most popular products?

The graph has been updated to highlight our top 5 most popular products. Popularity was measured by the amount of growth in purchase order volume over the past 12 months. This list includes health and beauty, housewares, computer accessories, auto, and construction tools.

Some interesting insights to draw:

  • Computer accessories is extremely volatile, but at it's peak owned the largest market share
  • Construction tools has seen the most growth by percentage, but still represents a small market share
  • Auto products orders are the most consistent and stable of the group

Are there any seasonal trends?

The graph has been updated to solely look at last year's holday season, October to January. This provides large scale trends across our entire product inventory. Understanding these major swings can help us allocate resources, adjust product inventory, and forecast finances.

Insights:

  • A major upswing in orders for November
  • A downward trend into December, returning order volumes to normal
  • Another spike in Jaunary, but not as significant as in November

Conclusion

The previouse examples showcase how a user might interact and draw insights from the visualization. At this point, the graph displays all of our products, but you can filter to specific products by using the dropdown selection.
