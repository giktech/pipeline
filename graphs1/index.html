The charts below show the order delivery time for each state (chart 1) and each product category (chart 2). Delivery timelines outside the normal range (0-500 hours) are in red.


Clicking on a bar in the chart creates a brushable area that be used to limit the data range (states) for the product categories chart below. The brush on chart1 (to select states) can be dragged on both edges.It can also be moved horizontally through the span of the chart. Reloading the page will reset both the charts to original form.
